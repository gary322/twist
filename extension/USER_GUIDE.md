## Installation

### Chrome & Edge
1. Visit the [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/twist)
2. Click "Add to Chrome" or "Add to Edge"
3. Confirm the installation when prompted
4. The TWIST icon will appear in your browser toolbar

### Firefox
1. Visit [Firefox Add-ons](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/twist)
2. Click "Add to Firefox"
3. Grant the necessary permissions
4. The TWIST icon will appear in your toolbar

### Safari
1. Download TWIST from the [Mac App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/twist)
2. Open Safari Preferences ‚Üí Extensions
3. Enable TWIST Browser Extension
4. The icon will appear in your Safari toolbar

## Troubleshooting

### Extension Not Working?

1. **Check if the extension is enabled:**
   - Chrome: chrome://extensions
   - Firefox: about:addons
   - Make sure TWIST is toggled ON

2. **Refresh the page:**
   - Some features require a page refresh after installation

3. **Check your internet connection:**
   - The extension needs internet to sync data

### Not Earning Tokens?

1. **Verify the site is a publisher:**
   - Look for the purple TWIST badge
   - Not all websites are verified publishers

2. **Check your activity:**
   - You need to actively engage with content
   - Idle tabs don't earn tokens

3. **Privacy mode:**
   - Ensure you're not in strict privacy mode
   - Check that the site isn't blocked

### Wallet Issues?

1. **Connection problems:**
   - Make sure your Solana wallet extension is installed
   - Try disconnecting and reconnecting

2. **Transaction failures:**
   - Check you have enough SOL for gas fees
   - Ensure you have sufficient TWIST balance

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How much can I earn per day?**
A: Earnings depend on your browsing activity. Active users typically earn 50-200 TWIST daily.

**Q: Is the extension free?**
A: Yes! The TWIST extension is completely free to use.

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.

### Earning Questions

**Q: Why am I not earning on YouTube?**
A: You earn on YouTube if the channel owner is a verified TWIST publisher.

**Q: Do I earn in private/incognito mode?**
A: No, the extension doesn't track activity in private browsing mode.

**Q: Can I earn on multiple devices?**
A: Yes, install the extension on all your devices and sign in with the same account.

### Staking Questions

**Q: What's the minimum stake amount?**
A: The minimum stake is 10 TWIST tokens.

**Q: Can I unstake anytime?**
A: Stakes have a 30-day lock period. After that, you can unstake freely.

**Q: How is APY calculated?**
A: APY is based on the influencer's performance, engagement, and tier.

**Q: What happens to my stake if an influencer becomes inactive?**
A: Your stake remains safe. You can unstake after the lock period.

### Security Questions

**Q: Is my browsing data private?**
A: Yes, we only track engagement on verified sites. Personal data is never collected.

**Q: Is my wallet secure?**
A: The extension never has access to your private keys. All transactions require your approval.

**Q: Can I disable tracking?**
A: Yes, use the privacy settings to control or disable tracking.
